Cichlids often use visual cues to recognize and choose mates. Lake Victoria is a lake in East Africa. Due to pollution and loss of planktivorous fish, it has increased turbidity (due to algae). In lake Victoria, this has led to a loss of species barriers among closely related species of cichlids, which now hybridize. You are curious whether fish in Lake Malawi faces a similar threat if the water there becomes less clear. You set up two mesocosms, one with turbid water and one with clear water. After 30 days, you remove all adults and grow the offspring to the point that you can assign them as being of pure origin (both parents species one or both parents species two) or hybrids between species one and two.
