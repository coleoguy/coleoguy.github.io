Day 0

R is a free software environment and programming language designed for statistical analyses, but it is also a very powerful environment for data exploration and visualization. RStudio is an IDE (Integrated Development Environment) - basically a program that makes it easier to code. Both of these programs work on MacOS, Windows, and Unix.

Prior to our first meeting please install these programs.

Installing R

  • Go to the R homepage and click download R.
  • Pick a mirror that is in Texas or at least in the United States.
  • Select the correct version for your system and follow the prompts.
Installing RStudio
  • Go to the RStudio homepage and click on the download link below the free version of RStudio Desktop.
  • Select the correct version for your system and follow the prompts.
Once both programs are installed you can simply open RStudio (R will start automatically).
