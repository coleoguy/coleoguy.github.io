R Packages

DirectRepeateR

Find and annotate direct repeats in genome assemblies; produces GFF-style output.

chromePlus

Markov models for chromosome number and binary trait evolution, compatible with diversitree.

micRocounter

Identify all 2-6mer repeats in genome assemblies, optimized for speed.

evobiR

R functions for empirical data analysis, simulation, and teaching. Vignette

SAGA2
